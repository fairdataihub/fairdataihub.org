## 📝 Event Description

The **2026 Bridge2AI Spring All-Hands Meeting** brings together the Bridge2AI consortium and the broader biomedical research community for a two-day meeting at the NIH Neuroscience Center. Chaired by Dr. Mónica Muñoz Torres and Dr. Yulia Levites Strekalova, this year's meeting is themed **"From Data to Wisdom"**, reflecting the program's evolution from foundational data infrastructure toward tangible results and a broader collaborative community.

The NIH Common Fund's **Bridge to Artificial Intelligence (Bridge2AI)** program was established to address the AI-readiness gap in biomedical data and promote the ethical use of AI in behavioral and medical research. Now in its fourth year, the meeting will highlight landmark data releases and tools developed across the consortium, as well as contributions from early-career researchers through poster presentations and talks.

Bridge2AI is extending an open invitation to the broader community. If you work in biomedical informatics, AI/ML for healthcare, translational data science, or clinical research, you are welcome to attend. More information and registration are available on the [meeting website](https://bridge2ai.org). Registration closes April 15, 2026.
