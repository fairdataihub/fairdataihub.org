## 📝 Event Description

The **NativeBio Data Consortium** is hosting the final **AI READI & Tribal Sovereignty Conversations on Artificial Intelligence in Research** meeting. This public event brings together researchers, tribal leaders, and community members to discuss the intersection of artificial intelligence, data sovereignty, and the rights of Indigenous communities in the context of biomedical research. Topics include interdisciplinary team science, treaty rights, ophthalmological screening for Type 2 diabetes, and data security and human subjects protections in ML/AI research.

This meeting is open to the public. Lunch will be provided.

## 💡 Our Role / Contribution

**Bhavesh Patel** will co-present a talk titled **"AIREADI Data Security and Human Subjects Protections Challenges in ML/AI"** alongside Shannon McWeeney, Ph.D. (Oregon Health & Science University).
